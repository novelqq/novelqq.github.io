Opcode Ivory

🔍

Remember those techwear cosplay photos of Japanese girls wearing gunpla headgear and gas masks and things like that? Some Patlabor Alphonse got in here as well. Started as a tablescrap with the ultra cursed wario arm tech in the hips but I was motivated to finish it because other people threatened to create more monstrosities with startling celerity. Otherwise I would've been a little hesitant to post this on my accounts for obvious reasons. Everything in the build is lego including the panties (Scala), but not the shirt, which is a non-lego doll garment that I bought from eBay many years ago. I'm sure there's a neat scala shirt that would have worked just as well but the grey monotones sort of callback those funny headgear cosplay photos where they would wear simple undergarments and calvin kleins lmao. I also color corrected the big mac at the tummy to match light nougat, in case anyone is wondering. I bought the unicorn castle just to get the light nougat 1x1 round bricks, which roughly doubled the total amount of light nougat bricks I own.
